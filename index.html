About Us

Where we're based

We're based in Hatfield/st albans region, but soon to spread our wings as we begin to gig further out to many regions across the country.

Music Genre

Our music is a mix between classic rock, pshycodelic blues and progressive rock with a mix match of both of folk, jazz and classical in our playing from time to time.

The Future

Our plans for the future are to play at as many venues as possible; increase our fan base, and create great music for our fans and listeners all in order to increase our chances of radio play, liklihood of playing at festivals, and to potentially make a living out of something we love doing through hardwork and determination.
